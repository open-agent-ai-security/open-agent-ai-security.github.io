Marketo campaign tracking

Marketing email blasts are auto-detected here whenever tokenized email traffic (Marketo mkt_tok links) spikes past 50 hits/hour. Almost all of that volume is corporate email-security infrastructure (Microsoft Safe Links, Mimecast, Proofpoint, …) auto-fetching every link — not people — so we hold it out of the reach figures above and log each send's real human click-through below. New campaigns append here as they land.

Detected campaignTrackedHumanScannersCTR
📧 2026-07-01 · Marketo blast11,08712110,9661.1%
Human click-throughs (est.)121 · 1.1%
Automated scanner fetches10966 · 98.9%

A ~1.1% human-signal rate — recipients showing a mobile viewport or a time-separated revisit — is a healthy click-through for a B2B email send; the rest is unavoidable scanner traffic every blast generates. How each pageview is attributed (Marketo token → mobile / revisit / burst-hour screen) is documented at the top of stats/generate.py.
